## 6. Revision & Reinforcement Cycles

Every newly-studied topic is revisited on a **1-day → 3-day → 7-day → 14-day** schedule. Saturdays host a **3-hour full mock + review** to consolidate the week. The final 10 days before the exam shift to **80% revision / 20% mock practice**.

## 7. Adaptation Playbook

| Trigger | Action |
|---|---|
| Missed a session | Roll the missed block onto Saturday's buffer slot; do not skip the next-day revision. |
| Quiz/mock < 60% on a topic | Insert a 30-minute remediation block within 48 hours, and re-add a 7-day revision touch. |
| Ahead of schedule | Pull the next-rank topic forward; do not extend session length. |
| Sick/blocked day | Drop one revision pass before dropping any new theory; recompute weekly hours. |
